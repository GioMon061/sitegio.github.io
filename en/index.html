How I can help you

Founder Communication Strategy is the first solid step to gain clarity.
In two 60-minute sessions, we look at how you communicate your business externally (profile, messaging and overall direction) and identify what’s working, what’s holding you back, and which path actually makes sense for you, without starting from standard solutions.

Request info

There isn’t a single way to work together, because not all companies are at the same stage.
My services cover different levels of involvement: from strategic communication direction, to building a solid brand system and ongoing support alongside the founder.
The goal is always the same: to make the business clear, coherent and credible externally, without wasting time or relying on disconnected interventions.

Social Master

Custom social media design subscription for companies without a dedicated team.

You are losing clients and sales because your social media doesn’t grab attention. Social Master transforms your profiles into a growth engine, with tailored content that makes you visible and competitive.


What’s included

• Up to 15 pieces of content per month: a customised, flexible mix of graphics, carousels, and short videos, optimised for your company’s key platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.).
• Scalable social brand identity: if a structured brand identity is lacking, a basic visual identity will be developed to grow with your business. If you already have one, each piece of content will be aligned with your brand standards.
• GPT Bonus for ready-to-use ideas and content (to be used in combo):
→ Content Ideas GPT: tailored prompts to generate relevant and strategic content ideas for your sector, customised to your business’ needs, so you never run out of topics.
→ Super Content GPT: based on ideas generated by Content Ideas GPT, this prompt assists you in creating effective, targeted posts ready for your main platforms.
• Monthly planning with up to 2 revisions, ensuring you always have prepared, strategic content, even without a dedicated team.
• Communication and support via email, Google Meet, and Slack, for an organised and transparent workflow.

Pack & Merch

Packaging and merchandising to stand out and attract customers.

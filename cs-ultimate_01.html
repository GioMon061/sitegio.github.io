This social media graphic's purpose was encouraging the audience to download the free guide by giving them some info, and of course, keeping the consistency with the company's brand.

The first step has been obviously reading the guide, and taking notes of the key concepts to be included on each slide.

While the company's brand guideline has more colors than the ones used here, I decided to narrow down the colors to the same ones used in the guide itself in order to highlight the consistency (for example, even though purple is one of the company's brand colors, it is never used in this guide, and I did the same with the teaser).

Following the same reasoning, I chose similar geometric patterns, albeit different from the ones used in the guide in order to keep simplicity and ease of reading from mobile devices.

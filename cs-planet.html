planet brochure concept.

category:

Brochure Design

tools/languages:

Adobe InDesign, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator

description:

Brochure Design concept for an integrated aerospace and data analytics company.

The goal of the project was to creatively re-design an existing printable brochure, sticking to the same text, and keeping the company's brand colors (teal and grey), as well as their chosen font.

I wanted to highlight the concept that Planet provides with these beautiful satellite images by using a circle to mimic the Earth, as well as functioning as a reminder of the company's name.

At the same time, I wanted to use one of their photos for this purpose, hence I ended up thinking to use a circle mask, split on 2 pages, which reveals one of their photos below, and with the text of the brochure that follows the circle shape. I chose to use the same approach for the title of the brochure (a mask, revealing the image below).

Finally, I chose the image very carefully, as I was not just looking for much brighter colors compared to the previous brochure, but also colors that could somehow match/compliment the teal of the companyâ€™s brand.

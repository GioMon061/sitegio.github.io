luxury presence instagram style guide concept.

category:

Social Media Graphic Design

tools/languages:

Figma, Adobe Photoshop

description:

Concept for an Instagram style guide.

The goal for this concept was to create a style guide for Luxury Presence’s Instagram feed so that posts could feel harmonious as they did not have a cohesive style. As a result, they had inconsistencies in the colors, typography, position of logos, and overall flow and story-telling of the feed.
Since the company mostly used Figma for most of their graphics, this has been my tool of choice too, as well as Photoshop. In order to give an overall consistency with the brand, I used the same color palette of LP’s website, especially the dark blue and the sand.
One of the biggest challenges, though, was keeping consistency while considering all the different kinds of media they display in their Instagram’s feed: real estate properties images, real estate videos, press mentions, quotes for clients, and finally, photos/images of both clients and team members, all sharing a huge range of colors. This is why I chose to add the same border to all of them and to make specific posts templates for featuring images of team members and clients respectively.
Finally, in the full comp, I also added a guide about the color palette and the usage of the 2 company’s fonts.

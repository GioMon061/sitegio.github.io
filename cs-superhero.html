Illustration about what a superhero should look like.

I started to think about what a “real-life” superhero would be: everyone who does extraordinary –but even ordinary things- in hard, sometimes terrible circumstances… that’s a superhero for me. I googled some images for inspiration, and saw a woman in a wheelchair… and this is how this illustration came to life. I chose a light purple for the skin because the superhero could be anyone.

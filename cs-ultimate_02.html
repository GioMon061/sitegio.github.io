description:

Eventbrite graphics to introduce the webinar about the "Director’s Guide To Customer Obsessed Support Teams in eCommerce".

The goal of these graphics was encouraging the registration to the webinar based on the same free guide, through one cover image and three descriptive ones.

I did already had the chance to read the guide when I made the LinkedIn Teaser (here) for the same guide, so the key info were already clear. The images were supposed to complement the text of the actual text description of the event.

I tried to slightly change my approach here, and while I kept the same colors and images of the guide, I only used one of the two brand's fonts, and highlighted the key sentences instead of underline them so that they would have “popped” more since apparently Eventbrite tends to scale down images.

planted schitzel ad concept.

category:

AD Design

tools/languages:

Adobe Illustrator

description:

Design a DIN A4 advertise for planted.schnitzel launch.

The goal of the project was to create a brand consistent ad design for the launch of the new planted schnitzel for this company, which makes plant-based meat.

It was of course necessary to keep both the logo and the brand's colors. I also chose to keep the same font always for consistency reasons, used one of their promotional images for on of the ad -adjusted with Photoshop- and designed all “pig” graphics in Illustrator for both ads, whose purpose was showing a happy pig, since the ad was playing with the term pig with the sentence "no pig deal".

For inspiration, I checked both the website and their Instagram account, and tried to stick to the brand’s look and feel as much as possible.

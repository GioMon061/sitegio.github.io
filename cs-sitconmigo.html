sit conmigo concept.

category:

Web Design

tools/languages:

Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop

description:

Responsive web design concept for a furniture company specializing in chairs that are produced sustainably and ethically.

The goals of the client were 3: showcase the chairs and entice customers to pre-order them; highlight her mission as a fair trade and fair labor designer; visually communicate the personality and mission of the her business. I took inspiration from similar furniture websites with a sleek look and feel, and an easy access to contacts. Showing the mission was also important to highlight how Sit Conmigo paid particular attention to sustainability. Three comps have been realised for the project: desktop, tablet and mobile.

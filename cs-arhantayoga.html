description:

Landing page re-design concept for this Yoga online course.

The goal of the project was to creatively re-design their landing page, maintaining as much as possible the current structure. They wanted it to be more modern, calmer, and more user-friendly, an upgrade of the current page.

There was no brand style guide, but color palette was provided, as well as the official logo. I decided to change the typography with two sans-serif fonts that I felt were more appropriate for a yoga/wellness website. I also decided to use less images and some organic graphics/patterns, which in my opinion were more fitting to the look and feel they were aiming for.
